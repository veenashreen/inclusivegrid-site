Our Mission

We create ethical, AI-driven technologies designed from the ground up with and for neurodivergent people and people with disabilities. Our tools empower users, reduce digital barriers, and promote equity through thoughtful, inclusive design.

What We’re Building

NeuroAssist

An AI productivity tool tailored for ADHD and executive function support — smart reminders, chunked tasks, calming UI.

TalkTiles

Visual communication boards powered by AI — helping non-verbal users express themselves clearly and confidently.

InclusiveUI SDK

A toolkit for developers to easily add neurodiverse-friendly modes, focus settings, and accessible navigation patterns.

Bias Scanner AI

Identify hidden bias and accessibility issues in digital platforms using our open-source bias detection engine.

Why It Matters

Over 1 billion people globally live with a disability, and 1 in 7 are neurodivergent. InclusiveGrid exists to ensure they are not just included, but empowered — with technology that works for everyone, not just the average user.
